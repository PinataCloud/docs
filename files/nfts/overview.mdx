NFT Backup pins all IPFS content from your NFT collections to your Pinata account.

## How It Works

1. **Connect** - Add your wallet address
2. **Sync** - Pinata fetches your NFTs from OpenSea and extracts IPFS CIDs
3. **Backup** - Pin CIDs to your Pinata account
