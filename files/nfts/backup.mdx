---
title: "Backup"
description: "Pin your NFT CIDs to Pinata"
---

After syncing a wallet, backup the IPFS CIDs to your Pinata account.

## Backup All

To backup all CIDs for a wallet:

1. Go to [NFT Backup](https://app.pinata.cloud/nft-backup/wallets)
2. Select a wallet
3. Click **Backup All**

Pinata pins all CIDs in the background.

## Auto-Backup

Enable auto-backup to automatically pin new CIDs when you sync:

1. Select a wallet
2. Toggle **Auto-Backup** on

## Backup Status

Each CID shows its status:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Backed Up | Pinned to your account |
| Pending | Backup in progress |
| Not Backed Up | Not yet pinned |
